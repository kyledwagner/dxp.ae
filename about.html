What We Do
Stories can express the most complex ideas in the simplest way.
We’re here to help bring that story to life.

Our dedicated feature film department has been established to service independent and studio productions. DXP specializes in facilitating as well as developing feature films from start to finish.

We are driven by the unreasonable power of creativity and collaboration with our clients. We believe the best work thrives in culture and communication.

We are obsessed with the quality for documentaries and believe in the importance of a good story told with style.

DXP animations is a diverse team of artists and creative directors. From a hand drawn to photorealistic CGI, we bring ideas to life through the use of movement, storytelling and timing.

We have a full range of carefully selected photographers, tailoring to your requirements and budget.

How We Work
We are a production company for those driven by the unreasonable power of creativity.
