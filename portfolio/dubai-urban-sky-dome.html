The Client
The first in the world for urban aerial eco-system. Creating a Dubai Urban SkyDome Platform for aerial vehicles under 500 ft. operations.
It’s an intelligent eco-system with digital platform for instant authorizations, virtual AirMaps, air services and governances. Creating an environment that will promote potential new Urban Aerial Vehicle business opportunities and models such as door-to-door deliveries by operated by the public and individuals using their own Drones and any other Urban Aerial Vehicle. Introduce pre-defined spaces for clearance, take-off and landing.
