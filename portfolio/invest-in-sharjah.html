The Client
Sharjah Investment and Development Authority are dedicated to the continued transformation and economic diversification of the Emirate and are committed to guiding foreign investors to the tremendous opportunity and potential that Sharjah offers.
Invest in Sharjah facilitates the investment process by fostering close collaboration between the public and private sector. By bringing investors and opportunities together they fuel growth and prosperity, and streamline an end-to-end solution to investing in the emirate.

The Task
To develop a contemporary and long lasting campaign film, representing the core values of Invest in Sharjah.
