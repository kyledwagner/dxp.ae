The Client
Ministry of Health & Prevention UAE key goals are to enhance community health by providing comprehensive, innovative, and fair healthcare services as per international standards.
MOH wanted to change the perceptions of the Emirati community in general & the Emirati youth specifically about Nursing and to encourage them to consider Nursing as a profession.

Development of a brand and social media campaign to promote nursing as a career path.
